Create.

EventHub is a place designed to easily create and display your campus events. Gone is the calamity of the bulliten board, with EventHub you can post your events to anyone on campus.

Discover.

Whether you're new on campus or an ingrained RSO, EventHub allows you to easily search for school events. You can look up events by interests like hiking or academics, or even by extras such as free food or giveaways.

Organize.

EventHub allows you to organize your campus life, you have a feed full of your event cards; each listing important information for each event. As an RSO you can easily track and edit your events as well.
